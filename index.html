About Me


Hey! I’m an Economics graduate with a passion for software engineering, data science, and automation. While my academic background lies in finance and data analysis, my real excitement comes from building innovative solutions that streamline processes and solve complex problems. From my early days of coding bots to optimize reselling opportunities, to developing automation tools that saved hundreds of manual hours at a major global corporation, I’ve consistently sought out ways to merge technology with real-world challenges.

Throughout my career, I’ve had the opportunity to work across a range of industries and technologies. My professional journey includes roles in financial analysis, and executive search (data engineering), where I’ve applied programming languages like Python, SQL, and VBA to automate workflows, improve decision-making, and deliver impactful results. My experience spans financial modeling, statistical analysis, and even developing machine learning models in PyTorch and SciKit Learn. Whether it’s building financial automation systems, implementing machine learning models, or developing web-based applications, I thrive on creating solutions that are efficient, scalable, and impactful.

I’m also deeply motivated by the idea of continuous learning and collaboration. I believe that technology is best leveraged when we work together, combining diverse perspectives to drive innovation. Whether I’m pairing with a colleague to optimize code, or exploring new technologies in my spare time, I’m always looking for opportunities to grow and make a difference.
