About Me

From my early days of coding bots to optimize reselling opportunities, to developing automation tools that saved hundreds of manual hours at a major global corporation, I’ve consistently sought out ways to merge technology with real-world challenges.

My experience spans financial modeling, statistical analysis, and even developing machine learning models in PyTorch. Whether it’s building financial automation systems, implementing machine learning models, or developing web-based applications, I thrive on creating solutions that are efficient, scalable, and impactful.
I’m also deeply motivated by the idea of continuous learning and collaboration. I believe that technology is best leveraged when we work together, combining diverse perspectives to drive innovation. Whether I’m pairing with a colleague to optimize code, or exploring new technologies in my spare time, I’m always looking for opportunities to grow and make a difference.
